01 / The question

Is the evidence consistent with the claim — claimant, vehicle, place and time?

A phone alone doesn't prove an accident. Merklit proves individual facts and combines independent facts to support, contradict or qualify the incident narrative — then routes weak evidence to human review, never automatic rejection.

01

Reduce ambiguity

Separate verified observations from claimant statements and unavailable evidence.

02

Strengthen provenance

03

Support review

Route contradictions and weak evidence to human review instead of automatic claim decisions.

02 / Proof model

Five assertions,
reported independently.

A weak signal is never converted into an unjustified statement that an incident definitely occurred.

A·01

Policyholder presence

The authenticated policyholder's enrolled device produced acceptable evidence from the claimed region and time.

OTP · device binding · location predicate · attestation

A·02

Media captured here

A photo or video was freshly captured inside the controlled workflow at the reported location and remained unchanged.

camera-only capture · content hash · signed manifest

A·03

Surveyor visited

The assigned, authenticated field representative produced visit evidence at the vehicle or property.

roster identity · arrival proof · guided inspection

A·04

Vehicle event

A vehicle-linked source reported a collision-like event at a particular time and location.

OEM · fleet · OBD · telematics correlated to VIN

A·05

Incident corroborated

Independent evidence sources are mutually consistent with the submitted incident account.

presence · fresh media · vehicle event · external records

03 / How it works

From claim to sealed evidence in five steps.

STEP 1

Configure

The insurer sets the claim template, region, evidence checklist, retention and assurance target.

STEP 2

Initiate

A case, expiring capture session and one-time server challenge are created — old evidence can't be replayed.

STEP 3

Capture

The active channel collects permitted identity, media, location, time and device evidence — hashed at capture.

STEP 4

Verify

Merklit validates signatures, freshness, consistency, duplicates and policy rules across all sources.

STEP 5

Decide

The insurer receives assertions, reason codes, selective proof and manual-review tools.

Default response

Insurers learn whether the claimed region and evidence requirements were supported — never an unrestricted dump of personal location history.

05 / Integrity, correctly framed

Tamper-evident by design.
Honest about what that means.

THE PROOF LAYER GUARANTEES

→The exact evidence commitment existed by a recorded block.

→A later change would produce a different hash.

→The public anchor can be checked independently of Merklit.

IT NEVER CLAIMS

✕That GPS or a sensor input was truthful.

✕That the claimant personally held the device.

✕That an accident occurred merely because a hash exists.

ENCRYPTION

AES-256-GCM per evidence object, tenant KMS keys, optional BYOK.

ON-CHAIN POLICY

PII, raw media and coordinates never enter the blockchain.

DISCLOSURE

Regional results by default; full reports require audited escalation.

LEGAL PACKAGE

CCA timestamps, Base anchoring and a Section 63 certificate workflow.
